Aphasia Support Group of Rochester


About the Group: Our group consists of individuals experiencing communication difficulties following an acquired brain injury such as a stroke. The group is open to families, friends, caregivers of loved ones with aphasia and other local professionals including speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists and physical therapists. The group has been meeting monthly since October 2012.

When we meet: The third Thursday of every month at 4:30 pm. The group lasts approximately an hour and a half.

Where we meet: Our location rotates between St.Johns Home and Nazareth College. Join us for our next meeting!
